Mission Profile

The difference between a weak negotiator and a strong one is often $30K+ per year — compounding over your career. Compensation Intelligence arms you with the data you need to negotiate what you're actually worth.

What's the real salary range for this role at this company? What do people at your level actually take home — base, bonus, equity? How does their offer compare to competitors? Walk into negotiations knowing exactly what to ask for.

Intelligence Categories

  • 1
    Base Salary Ranges

    Percentile breakdowns (25th, 50th, 75th, 90th) for the specific role at this company and market comparables.

  • 2
    Total Compensation

    Base + bonus + equity (RSUs/options) + other cash components like signing bonuses.

  • 3
    Benefits Package

    Health insurance quality, 401k match, PTO policies, parental leave, perks, and non-cash benefits.

  • 4
    Equity Analysis

    Vesting schedules, refresh grants, current stock performance, dilution risk (for startups).

  • 5
    Negotiation Intelligence

    Historical negotiation flexibility, typical counter-offer ranges, components most likely to move.
