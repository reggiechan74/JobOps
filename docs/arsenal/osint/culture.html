Mission Profile

Toxic culture doesn't appear in job postings. It shows up after you've signed, when you're working 60-hour weeks, dealing with political backstabbing, or watching colleagues burn out. Culture Intelligence reveals the reality behind the employer brand.

The Culture Intelligence agent analyzes what it's actually like to work at the target company. Beyond the marketing speak, it uncovers real employee experiences, management styles, work-life balance realities, and cultural red flags that could make or break your job satisfaction.
