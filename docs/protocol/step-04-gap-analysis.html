Gap Analysis Briefing

Identify capability gaps and create targeted study guides before committing to engagement.

/briefing

What It Does

Based on your assessment results, this command generates a comprehensive study guide to address identified skill gaps. It helps you make an informed decision: invest in preparation and engage, or disengage and conserve resources.

Two Operating Modes

Gaps Only Mode

Focused study guide targeting only identified weaknesses. Efficient for strong candidates with minor gaps.

/briefing Assessment.md JobPost.md gaps-only

Full Preparation Mode

Comprehensive interview readiness guide covering all aspects of the role.

/briefing Assessment.md JobPost.md 3d

Time-Aware Priority System

Briefings adapt to your available preparation time:

FOCUS NOW

Critical gaps that must be addressed — highest ROI for limited time

IF TIME PERMITS

Important but secondary — address after critical items

SKIM ONLY

Nice-to-have knowledge — includes acknowledgment scripts for gaps

Prep Time Options

  • +
    Days: 1d, 2d, 3d

    Short-term intensive preparation with prioritized action items.

  • +
    Weeks: 1w, 2w

    Extended preparation with deeper learning paths and practice exercises.

  • +
    Day-by-Day Schedule

    Specific study plan broken down by day with time allocations.

Briefing Contents

  • +
    Gap Analysis Summary

    Clear overview of identified weaknesses from assessment.

  • +
    Learning Resources

    Curated links to documentation, tutorials, and courses — researched for relevance.

  • +
    Practice Questions

    Sample interview questions targeting each gap area.

  • +
    Acknowledgment Scripts

    For gaps you can't fully address: honest, professional ways to discuss limitations.

Usage Example

$ /briefing Assessment_TechCorp.md TechCorp_SeniorDev.md 3d

Decision Point

After reviewing your briefing, you face a critical decision:

ENGAGE

Gaps are addressable with available prep time → Proceed to Strike Package Assembly

DISENGAGE

Gaps are too significant or time-prohibitive → Return to Target Acquisition
