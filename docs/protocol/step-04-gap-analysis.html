Should You Even Apply?

Find out in 5 minutes. Know exactly what you'd need to learn, how long it would take, and whether it's worth your time.

/briefing

What It Does

You just scored 73/100 on a job assessment. Should you apply? This command tells you exactly what you're missing, how long it would take to credibly fill those gaps, and whether you should walk away instead. No more wasting 40 hours prepping for a role you'll never get.

Some gaps are dealbreakers. Some are easy wins. The difference between wasting time on a hopeless application and investing in a winnable one is knowing which gaps matter — and whether they're closeable in your timeline.

Two Operating Modes

Gaps Only Mode

For 85+ scores: You're already qualified. Get a laser-focused 2-day study plan for the 2-3 skills you're missing. Spend your time where it matters.

/briefing Assessment.md JobPost.md gaps-only

Full Preparation Mode

For 70-84 scores: You're borderline. Get a complete crash course covering technical gaps, interview questions, and honest acknowledgment scripts for gaps you can't close in time.

/briefing Assessment.md JobPost.md 3d

Time-Aware Priority System

Not all gaps are created equal. The system categorizes every weakness by impact and learnability, then builds your study plan around what actually moves the needle:

FOCUS NOW

Critical dealbreakers or high-ROI gaps that can be closed with focused effort. These determine whether you get the offer.

IF TIME PERMITS

Important but not decisive. Address these after critical items if you have bandwidth. They strengthen your position but won't make or break you.

SKIM ONLY

Nice-to-have knowledge that won't meaningfully improve your odds. Includes scripts to professionally acknowledge limitations if asked.

Prep Time Options

  • +
    Days: 1d, 2d, 3d

    Fast-track intensive prep. The system prioritizes ruthlessly—you work on what matters most. Perfect for short-notice opportunities where you need maximum return on limited time.

  • +
    Weeks: 1w, 2w

    Extended preparation runway. Deeper learning paths, hands-on exercises, and practice projects. Build genuine competence, not just interview answers.

  • +
    Day-by-Day Schedule

    Concrete battle plan with hourly time allocations. No ambiguity about what to study when. Follow the plan and show up prepared.

Briefing Contents

  • +
    Gap Analysis Summary

    Blunt assessment of where you're weak. No sugarcoating. Each gap categorized by severity, learnability, and whether it's truly a dealbreaker.

  • +
    Learning Resources

    Curated links that actually teach the skill, not generic "intro to" courses. Researched for quality and relevance to this specific role's technical stack.

  • +
    Practice Questions

    Interview questions likely to expose each gap. Practice these so you're not blindsided. Includes model answers and common follow-ups.

  • +
    Acknowledgment Scripts

    Some gaps can't be closed in time. Here's how to discuss them honestly without disqualifying yourself. Turns weaknesses into learning opportunities.
