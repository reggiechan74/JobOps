Build Tailored Resume

HAM-Z methodology with provenance verification. Draft → Verify → Harden pipeline.

/buildresume

What It Does

This is the core resume generation pipeline. It executes a 3-step process that creates a targeted resume, verifies every claim against your career inventory, and hardens it against scrutiny.

The 3-Step Pipeline

1

Initial Draft

Creates targeted resume using HAM-Z methodology, selecting optimal achievements from your career inventory based on job requirements.

Step1_Draft_Resume_[Role]_[Company].md
2

Provenance Check

Analyzes every claim for credibility issues, evidence gaps, and risk factors. Identifies unbounded metrics, unsupported superlatives, and mechanism-free outcomes.

Step2_Provenance_Analysis_[Role]_[Company].md
3

Final Hardening

Incorporates all provenance recommendations to produce a defensible, interview-ready resume with zero fabricated claims.

Step3_Final_Resume_[Role]_[Company].md

HAM-Z Formula

Achieved [ Metric-Driven Result ] by leveraging [ Hard Skill ] to [ perform specific action ]
Example: "Achieved 40% reduction in deployment time by leveraging Kubernetes to implement automated CI/CD pipelines across 12 microservices"

Cultural Profiles

Resume style adapts to regional expectations:

  • +
    Canadian (Default)

    Quantified achievements, conservative language, specific technologies.

  • +
    US

    Action-oriented, competitive positioning, achievement-dense.

  • +
    UK

    Understated, competency-focused, clear progression narrative.

  • +
    European

    Education-prominent, language skills, mobility emphasis.
