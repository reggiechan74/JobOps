Walk In Ready

Interviewers will test every claim on your resume. Know exactly what they'll ask — and how to answer.

/interviewprep

What It Does

The interview is where resume claims go to die — or get proven. This command predicts what interviewers will ask based on your resume-job alignment, then arms you with strategic responses.

When you receive an interview callback, it generates customized questions that interviewers are likely to ask, prioritized by probability. Each question comes with STAR format coaching, answer strategies, and red flags to avoid.

Question Likelihood Tagging

Questions are prioritized by probability of being asked:

HIGH LIKELIHOOD

Core competency questions — prepare these thoroughly, expect them

MODERATE LIKELIHOOD

Supplementary questions — prepare but don't over-invest

LOW LIKELIHOOD

Edge case questions — skim, don't memorize

Question Components

  • +
    Technical Questions

    Predicted based on skills you listed and expertise you claimed. If you said "Kubernetes orchestration," expect "Walk me through a complex K8s deployment you designed."

  • +
    Behavioral Questions

    STAR format coaching for leadership, conflict, and achievement scenarios. Turns your resume bullets into interview-ready stories with strategic advantage.

  • +
    Follow-up Questions

    Interviewers always drill down. Anticipated follow-ups for each question, so you're never caught off-guard.

  • +
    Red Flags to Avoid

    Common mistakes that signal you oversold your resume. Know what NOT to say for each question type.

STAR Format Structure

S
Situation

Set the context — when, where, what was the challenge?

T
Task

What was your specific responsibility or goal?

A
Action

What did YOU do? (Use "I", not "we")

R
Result

Quantified outcome — metrics, impact, learning

Time-Aware Practice Schedule

Like briefings, interview prep adapts to your available time:

  • +
    1d — Rapid Prep

    Focus on HIGH likelihood questions only. Quick answer bullets.

  • +
    3d — Standard Prep

    HIGH + MODERATE questions. Full STAR responses drafted.

  • +
    1w+ — Deep Prep

    All questions. Mock interview practice schedule included.
