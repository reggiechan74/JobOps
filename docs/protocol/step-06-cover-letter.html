Generic cover letters get ignored. This one includes a requirements-matching table showing exactly how you fit.

/coverletter

What It Does

Most cover letters are forgettable fluff. "I'm excited about this opportunity..." They all sound the same. A requirements-matching table forces specificity — and proves you actually read the job description.

Generates a targeted cover letter from your hardened Step 3 resume. Unlike generic AI cover letters, this maintains the provenance chain — every claim can be traced back to your career inventory.

Key Components

  • +
    Requirements-Matching Table

    A clear visual mapping between job requirements and your qualifications with evidence citations.

  • +
    Provenance Chain

    Every claim references specific achievements from your hardened resume, maintaining defensibility.

  • +
    Strategic Narrative

    Connects your experience to the company's specific needs and challenges.

  • +
    Optional Personalization

    Address to specific hiring manager when name is provided.
