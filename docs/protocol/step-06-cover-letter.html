Prove You're Qualified — With Evidence

Generic cover letters get ignored. This one includes a requirements-matching table showing exactly how you fit.

/coverletter

What It Does

Most cover letters are forgettable fluff. "I'm excited about this opportunity..." They all sound the same. A requirements-matching table forces specificity — and proves you actually read the job description.

This cover letter does something most can't: prove every claim. Hiring managers can check your evidence table and verify you're not just saying what they want to hear.

Key Components

  • +
    They Asked, You Deliver

    Shows hiring managers you meet their requirements — with proof for each one. No vague claims, just evidence.

  • +
    Nothing Made Up

    Every claim traces back to your real experience. No fluff they can poke holes in during the interview.

  • +
    Answers "Why You?"

    Tells the story of why YOUR background fits THEIR specific problems. Not a generic template.

  • +
    Skip "Dear Hiring Manager"

    Use the hiring manager's name when you have it. Small touch, big difference in getting read.
