Stop Wasting Time on Lost Causes

Know your real chances before investing hours in an application. A 100-point assessment tells you if it's worth the fight.

/assessjob

What It Does

Every application takes hours. Tailoring the resume. Writing the cover letter. Preparing for interviews. What if you knew upfront that you had a 23% fit? Would you still apply?

This command generates a job-specific scoring rubric and evaluates your career inventory against it — giving you a clear, numerical answer to "Is this worth my time?" Not a gut feeling. Not hope. A 100-point assessment that tells you whether to engage or move on.

Score Interpretation

The score isn't just a number — it's a decision-making tool. Here's what it means for your strategy:

90-100 Strong Engage

Excellent fit — prioritize this opportunity and invest full effort

75-89 Engage

Good fit with minor gaps — proceed with tailored preparation

60-74 Consider

Moderate fit — only proceed if gaps are directly addressable

<60 Disengage

Poor fit — don't waste hours you'll never get back
