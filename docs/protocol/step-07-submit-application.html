Don't Let Formatting Sink You

Poor formatting gets resumes rejected before humans see them. Professional conversion ensures your content reaches hiring managers.

/convert or /formatresume

What It Does

40% of resumes are rejected by ATS before a human ever sees them — often due to formatting issues. JobOps converts your carefully crafted content into formats that both machines and humans can read.

Choose between Word DOCX for ATS compatibility or PDF for visual presentation. Both formats preserve your content while ensuring parsing systems can extract your qualifications correctly.

Two Conversion Options

/convert — Word DOCX

Strategic choice for online application portals. Most corporate ATS systems parse Word documents more reliably than PDFs. Uses pandoc for professional conversion with clean formatting that machines can extract correctly.

ATS-optimized Editable output Pattern matching

/formatresume — PDF

Strategic choice for direct submission to humans. When emailing hiring managers or uploading to systems that display documents visually, PDF preserves your exact formatting. Uses Playwright for pixel-perfect generation with professional themes.

Submission Strategy

After conversion, you're ready to submit. Remember:

  • Use DOCX for online application forms with parsing
  • Use PDF for direct email or uploads where formatting matters
  • Keep markdown source for future iterations
  • Track submission date for follow-up timing
