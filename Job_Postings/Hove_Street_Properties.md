About the job
Job Summary: Reporting to the COO, the position will be directly accountable for the real estate asset management duties of an assigned portfolio, working with Hove’s legal, leasing, lease administration, accounting and property management operations. Hove own and manage primarily industrial property across North America and offers a unique hands-on environment where an individual can make an impact while continuing to develop their professional skills. Based in the Toronto head office, the person in this role will be an integral member of the senior management group that oversees over 17M SF. The position requires applied industrial property experience, effective leadership & communications skills, and demonstrated experience in Leasing and Construction.

Job Responsibilities



Drive portfolio performance (e.g. occupancy / NOI) through active asset management and collaboration with leasing and operations.
In coordination with ownership & the senior executive team, establish key value enhancing strategies and oversee the execution of resulting initiatives.
Lead activity related to Leasing, including the lease negotiation process (Rent / TI’s) and collaborating with legal and property managers.
In coordination with the operations team (construction, property management, leasing, accounting), lead the development and implementation of annual business plans for the assigned portfolio and ensure deliverables are met.
Responsibilities to ensure all legal agreements (e.g. Leases / service contracts) are complete, current and support the objectives of the portfolio.
Responsible for co-development and implementation of physical capital plans.
Assist in the preparation of monthly, quarterly, and annual reporting for accounting/treasury and executives.
The individual will also be a key player in the acquisitions, dispositions and financing.
Ability to navigate challenging issues by coordinating the appropriate stakeholders to find an appropriate solution.
Monitor 3rd service providers in accordance with contract specifications, scope of services and performance.
Visit all properties in the portfolio on a regular basis to ensure appearance, maintenance & tenant satisfaction are in line with established standards

Minimum 7 years' related experience, with a minimum direct exposure to the industrial asset class of 2 years
Strong network and proven relationships in the commercial real estate industry (e.g. other owners / managers, Brokerages and 3rd party service providers).
Proficiency in Property Management software (MRI required), MS EXCEL, Word and PowerPoint is preferred; familiarity with ARGUS software is not required but would be a positive attribute

Strong customer service orientation coupled with excellent “hands-on” property management experience.
