# Canerector — Vice President, Real Estate: Meeting Notes (Markdown)

## Position Overview

The company seeks a Vice President of Real Estate to lead the strategic management of its industrial real estate portfolio. The role focuses on asset management, leasing, acquisitions, and stakeholder relations within a family-owned, diversified organization. The successful candidate will provide strategic guidance for maximizing the value of underutilized properties and optimizing current holdings across Canada and the US.

## Company Background

* The organization has grown its real estate operations organically, managing a portfolio of **105 properties**, with **35** leased to third parties and **70** operational facilities.
* The real estate division is the company’s **5th largest** business unit.
* The focus is on **acquisition, optimization, and strategic management** rather than property development.
* Recent activity includes **five operating company acquisitions** and a number of internal projects ranging from renovations to new builds.

## Key Responsibilities

### 1) Portfolio Strategy & Management

* Develop strategies for **excess land**, **underutilized properties**, and **leasing operations**.
* Collaborate with business leaders to manage **internal leasing** arrangements and advise on property-related decisions.
* Implement proposals to enhance the **profitability** and **value** of existing properties, including renovations and strategic disposals.

### 2) Leasing, Acquisitions & Dispositions

* Manage **renewals**, **new leases**, and **third-party tenant relations**.
* Evaluate opportunities for **acquisitions and dispositions** of industrial properties, focusing on long-term portfolio growth.
* Oversee leasing for a diverse portfolio that includes **self-storage**, **light industrial**, and **manufacturing** spaces.

### 3) Operational Oversight & Improvement

* Assess and improve operational efficiencies related to **property management**, **capital expenditures (CapEx)**, and **stakeholder relations**.
* Lead major **CapEx** initiatives, with input from local managers, and coordinate architectural or technical support as needed.

### 4) Stakeholder Engagement

* Build and maintain relationships with **internal business leaders**, **tenants**, **brokers**, and **municipal authorities**.
* Collaborate with the **board of directors** and **executive team** on property decisions.
* Act as the **primary contact** for third-party brokers and vendors.

## Ideal Candidate Profile

* **Experience:** Background in industrial real estate with a focus on leasing, acquisitions, and asset management.
* **Entrepreneurial & Practical Mindset:** Comfortable operating in a less structured environment, with the ability to propose new processes and strategies.
* **Strong Negotiation Skills:** Proven experience in leading negotiations for complex leasing and property transactions.
* **Strategic Thinker:** Ability to identify and propose profitable uses for land and properties, while maintaining focus on core business objectives.
* **Communication & Collaboration:** Excellent rapport-building skills with senior leaders, stakeholders, and tenants.
* **Hands-On & Flexible:** Willing to travel as needed to inspect properties, meet with brokers, and engage directly with stakeholders.
* **Bilingual:** French is an asset but not mandatory.

## Compensation & Benefits

* **Salary Range:** $180,000 to $200,000, depending on experience.
* **Bonus Structure:** Discretionary, based on contribution to profitability and strategic impact (**target 30%** of base salary).
* **Pension & Benefits:** **4% pension** contribution; standard health benefits (no coverage for eyewear/massage); **2× life insurance**.
* **Vacation & Work Structure:** **4 weeks** vacation; flexible with additional personal days; **hybrid** model (**Mon–Wed–Thu** in office).

## Growth & Development

* Support for **professional development** (courses, certifications, training).
* Access to a **junior staff** member (including administrative support) for day-to-day real estate tasks.
* Encouragement of **innovative ideas** and **strategic proposals**, with opportunities for increased responsibility and potential role expansion.

## Reporting Structure

* **Weekly 1:1s** with the Executive Team to discuss initiatives, priorities, and challenges.
* Regular updates with the **CEO**, **COO**, and **board members** to review strategic progress and operational performance.
* Collaboration with **legal**, **finance**, and **local management** teams as needed.

## Recruitment Process

* **Interviews:** Initial interview with **Matt**, followed by **Amanda**.
* **Background Checks:** Criminal record, driver’s abstract, and educational verification.
* **Start Date:** Current head of Real Estate (**Jonathan**) transitioning out in **early March**. Target start aligns with **February’s bonus cycle**, allowing the new hire to benefit from the **annual guaranteed bonus**.
