About Hearinglife Canada

HearingLife Canada, and National Affiliated Partners, is the largest provider of hearing healthcare service in Canada. Working in unison with the National Campaign for Better Hearing's awareness campaign (campaignforbetterhearing.org), our core philosophy is to of providing life-changing hearing care while increasing awareness of the effects of hearing loss and the benefits of hearing devices.

Across Canada, HearingLife consists of over 300 hearing healthcare clinics and nearly 900 dedicated professionals proudly united behind one simple vision, “To help more people hear better.”

THE OPPORTUNITY

Reporting directly to the VP of Finance and Administration, the Director, Facilities & Real Estate Operations is a key member of the Canadian Leadership Team. You will lead a team of five professionals overseeing new clinic development, relocations, remodels, facilities operations, and lease management across the national portfolio.

As the senior leader for real estate operations, you will also:


Present network and store development initiatives at executive and leadership meetings.
Provide weekly project updates to senior leadership and Head Office staff.
Continuously refine the national network and store development strategy to optimize growth, efficiency, and client experience.
Act as the primary liaison with HearingLife’s global real estate teams, ensuring alignment on concepts, standards, and best practices.
This is a high-impact role where you will influence strategic direction, drive operational excellence, and deliver results that shape the organization’s future.


Key Responsibilities

Leadership & Strategy


Lead and mentor a team of five professionals across project management, analytics, lease administration, and geo-mapping.
Develop and execute an annual roadmap of real estate and facilities initiatives, including relocations, remodels, greenfield developments, and renovation projects.
Present strategic and operational updates to executive leadership, ensuring visibility and alignment on network initiatives.
Partner with Sales, Finance, and global colleagues to maximize synergies and ensure portfolio optimization.
Continuously evaluate and refine network development strategies to adapt to market conditions and organizational priorities.


Network Analysis


Identify and prioritize new market opportunities to support HearingLife’s growth objectives.
Conduct network assessments to ensure balanced representation in key and emerging markets.
Perform demographic, socioeconomic, and competitive analyses to guide data-driven site selection and clinic placement.
Contribute to the CapEx budget process and track performance against strategic targets.


Project Execution – New Clinics, Remodels, Relocations, Restorations


Partner with brokers to identify optimal spaces and negotiate favorable lease terms.
Manage due diligence for new sites, including zoning, licensing, and regulatory compliance.
Oversee design and construction, ensuring all projects meet brand standards and are delivered on time and on budget.
Provide clear weekly project updates to leadership and Head Office teams, highlighting progress, risks, and mitigations.
Leverage project management tools (e.g., Asana) to manage timelines, streamline execution, and drive accountability.


Facilities Management


Direct all facilities repair and maintenance requests, setting budgets, service levels, and priorities.
Develop and manage a high-performing vendor network for cost-effective service delivery.
Implement preventative maintenance programs to extend asset life and ensure lease compliance.


Lease Administration


Build and maintain strong relationships with brokers, landlords, and property managers.
Negotiate lease renewals to secure favorable terms, including inducements, fixturing periods, equipment replacement, and rights of first refusal.
Maintain and report on critical lease data (expiry dates, renewal options, obligations) through LeaseEagle for proactive portfolio management.
